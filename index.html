### 📜 Visa Tips and Checklist 📜

1. Document Checklist — [https://www.vfsglobal.com/one-pager/switzerland/india/english/pdf/Checklist_Tourist-EN-2025.pdf](https://www.vfsglobal.com/one-pager/switzerland/india/english/pdf/Checklist_Tourist-EN-2025.pdf)
2. Get your 6 month bank statement on the last 2–3 days before your appointment and have it attested by the bank.
3. Your old passport isn't required if it has no active visas. Bring it as a precaution, but you likely won't need it.
4. Request separate leave and employment letters from your company.
5. The entire process took 1.5 hours. Arrived at 10:00 AM, finished at 11:30 AM. Document verification happens on the ground floor, payment on the first floor, then biometrics on the first floor.
6. Use your current address in the Visa Application Form, not your passport address. Your bank statement and employment letter serve as address proof.
7. Visa submitted Monday, approved and received by Friday — received within 5 days.
